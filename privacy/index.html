Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Dr. Jan Duffy, REALTOR® (License S.0197614.LLC), operating in connection with Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices Nevada Properties (“we,” “us”), collects and uses information when you visit skyesummithomes.com (the “Site”) or contact us about real estate services.

What information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact and inquiry information you submit through forms or email (for example, name, phone number, email address, and details about your real estate needs).
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, and general usage information, which hosting and analytics tools may log automatically.

How we use information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide real estate–related services, communicate with you about your requests, and improve the Site. If you submit information through a lead form, it may be processed by our CRM or related tools so we can follow up with you.

Cookies and similar technologies

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies from us or third-party providers (for example, analytics or embedded tools). You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some features work.

Third-party services

The Site may include features or links operated by third parties (for example, mapping tools, listing widgets, or social links). Those services have their own privacy practices; we encourage you to read their policies.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described above, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

You may opt out of certain marketing communications by following instructions in those messages or by contacting us using the information below. Nevada residents may have additional rights under applicable state law; you may contact us to inquire about rights that apply to you.

Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
