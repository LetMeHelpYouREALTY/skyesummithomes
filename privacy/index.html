Privacy Policy

What information we collect

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact and inquiry information you submit through forms or email (for example, name, phone number, email address, and details about your real estate needs).

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide real estate–related services, communicate with you about your requests, and improve the Site. If you submit information through a lead form, it may be processed by our CRM or related tools so we can follow up with you.

Third-party services

The Site may include features or links operated by third parties (for example, mapping tools, listing widgets, or social links). Those services have their own privacy practices; we encourage you to read their policies.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described above, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes, unless a longer period is required by law.

Your choices

You may opt out of certain marketing communications by following instructions in those messages or by contacting us using the information below. Nevada residents may have additional rights under applicable state law; you may contact us to inquire about rights that apply to you.
