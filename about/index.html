Why I Chose to Focus on Skye Summit

When Skye Summit launched in 2009, I saw something special: a master-planned community at 3,200 feet elevation with unmatched views, thoughtful design, and a lifestyle that appealed to discerning buyers. I made a decision to specialize—not be a general Vegas agent, but THE Skye Summit expert.
