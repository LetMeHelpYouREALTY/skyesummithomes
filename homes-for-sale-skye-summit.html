Skye Summit: New Construction Coming Fall 2027

Skye Summit is a 505-acre master-planned community in northwest Las Vegas—bordering Centennial Hills, just beyond the 215 Beltway—developed by Olympia Companies and the team behind Skye Canyon and Skye Hills. Planned for about 3,500 homes with desert-conscious design, integrated parks, and trail systems; the community is coming Fall 2027. KB Home’s Vertice at Skye Summit (299 homesites) may begin sales early 2027, and Woodside Homes is also planned among the first builders.
