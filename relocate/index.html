What Relocating Buyers Need to Know

Community Culture & Lifestyle Fit

Skye Summit has a distinct culture—active, social, lifestyle-focused. I'll help you understand if it's the right fit for your family.

HOA Process & Approval Timeline

HOA approval processes vary. I'll guide you through requirements, timelines, and what to expect.

Builder Timeline (If New Construction)

New construction timelines can be 6-12 months. I'll help you understand the process and coordinate with builders.

Financing Considerations

Out-of-state buyers face unique financing considerations. I'll connect you with lenders who understand your situation.

What to Expect

Differences from other markets: elevation, climate, lifestyle, community structure. I'll prepare you for what's different.

The Relocation Process

1

Virtual Tours of Available Homes

I'll provide detailed virtual tours, video walkthroughs, and live video calls so you can see homes remotely before visiting.

2

Community Orientation

Lifestyle overview, clubs, events, amenities. I'll help you understand Skye Summit's culture and what living here is really like.

3

Builder Coordination (If Applicable)

I'll coordinate with builders on your behalf, handle selections, timelines, and keep you informed throughout the process.

4

Timeline Management

Out-of-state logistics require careful planning. I'll manage timelines, coordinate inspections, and ensure everything stays on track.

5

Closing Coordination

Long-distance closing requires extra coordination. I'll handle all details so you can close remotely or with minimal travel.
